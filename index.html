Scorpio

About

Scorpio is the 8th astroligical sign in the Zodiac. The sun transits Scorpio from October 23rd to November 22nd. It's element is water and its quality is fixed. Scorpio's ruling planets include Mars and Pluto.

Scorpio Traits

Scorpio's strengths include being powerful, passionate, and brave. Their weaknesses include being distrusting, jealous, manipulative, and violent. They are assertive people with a lot of determination and focus. They are resourceful and fearless. They have a tendancy to be very secretive.
